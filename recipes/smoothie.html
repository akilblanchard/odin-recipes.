Tropical Smoothie

Description

Enjoy the delicious taste of tropical fruits all year round with this Tropical Smoothie recipe

Ingredients

  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup frozen mangoes
  • ½ cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • ½ cup frozen raspberries
  • ¼ teaspoon coconut extract
  • 1 teaspoon shredded coconut (optional topping)

instructions

  1. Add all ingredients together in a blender for about 10 seconds or until desired texture is reached.
  2. Pour into 2 cups. Top with additional fruit and a sprinkle of shredded coconut and serve.
